Why?
In the house where I grew up, there was an old Soviet film camera on a shelf and I was always curious about it. It still worked, and I loved hearing the sound of the shutter. When I moved to Pittsburgh for university, I brought this camera with me and got into film photography.

I primarily like to shoot urban scenes - particularly trains, bridges, and alleyways. On this page, I put some great photos that I've taken.
