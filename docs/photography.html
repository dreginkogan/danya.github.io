Why?
Growing up, my parents kept an old film camera from the Soviet Union on a shelf in our living room, and I was always curious about it. It still worked, and I loved hearing the sound of the shutter. When I moved to Pittsburgh for university, I brought this camera with me, and got into film photography.

On this page, I put some of my favorite photos that I took.
